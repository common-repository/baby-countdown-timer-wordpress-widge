Displays a baby countdown timer in your wordpress sidebar. 

== Description ==

Keep track of your pregnancy and allow family to keep track with this pregnancy countdown timer.  It will count down the days to your due date , show a progress graph and details on your baby's weight and size. MORE BABY WIDGETS: http://tripletsmommy.com/category/widgets

You simply input your due date (one time) and follow the simple directions to place the widget on your blog sidebar. The widget does the rest! We designed this widget to fit nicely in the sidebar of most blog designs.

== Installation ==

This section describes how to install the plugin and get it working.


1. Upload `Baby_Countdown_Widget_WP.php` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. In your admin go to the new admin link "Baby COuntDown Timer"
4. Follow the instructions there
